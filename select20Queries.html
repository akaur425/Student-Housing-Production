Available Queries

  • Query 1: Students in same house

    Shows pairs of students who live in the same house along with the house owner.

  • Query 2: Rooms with assigned students

    Lists all rooms and the students assigned to them (if any).

  • Query 3: Students without assigned rooms

    Displays students who currently do not have any room assigned.

  • Query 4: Rooms above average price

    Shows rooms whose price is above the average price of all rooms.

  • Query 5: House occupancy count

    Displays each house and the number of rooms currently occupied, including the owner's name.

  • Query 8: Students with hobbies and interests

    Lists students along with their hobbies and interests.

  • Query 9: Rooms under $750, pets and smoking allowed

    Shows rooms priced under $750 where both pets and smoking are allowed.

  • Query 10: Students not studying in the same school

    Displays students who are the only ones in their school (no classmates).

  • Query 11: Person with highest rating

    Finds the person who has received the highest rating from feedback.

  • Query 12: Past contracts

    Shows contracts that have ended before a specific date, including student and owner names.

  • Query 13: Current contracts per owner

    Displays the number of active contracts for each house owner.

  • Query 14: Owners without contracts

    Lists family owners who currently do not have any active contracts.

  • Query 15: Rooms with no students

    Shows rooms that currently have no students assigned.

  • Query 16: Houses with at least one room available

    Lists houses that have at least one available room.

  • Query 17: Cheapest room in Surrey

    Finds the room with the lowest price in Surrey.

  • Query 18: Students with houses and schools in same city

    Shows students whose house and school are in the same city.

  • Query 19: Owners with more than 1 house

    Displays owners who own more than one house.

  • Query 20: Owners with most contracts

    Shows the owner(s) with the highest number of contracts.
